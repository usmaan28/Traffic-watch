About Us

Welcome to Traffic watch, where chaos meets order in the world of traffic!

Ever found yourself staring blankly at a traffic signal, wondering whether to stop, go, or just do a funky dance? Well, fear no more! At Traffic for u, we're on a mission to bring clarity to the chaos of traffic rules and signals.

Our team of experts, consisting of retired traffic cops, road enthusiasts, and a few pigeons who claim to have mastered the art of crossing the road safely, are here to guide you through the intricate web of traffic regulations.

Whether you're a seasoned driver, a novice cyclist, or just someone who enjoys watching traffic from the comfort of their own home, Traffic for u has something for everyone!

So buckle up, grab your favorite traffic cone, and embark on this wild journey with us. Remember, at Traffic for u, we don't just follow the rules; we make the rules!
